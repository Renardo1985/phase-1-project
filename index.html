Welcome to Iconic Desserts!

A one woman team, that specializes in bespoke cakes and treats with a devoted attention to detail and a passion for creating edible art. I pride myself in my personalized design service and work with my customers to create the perfect desserts for their special occasion. I am truly passionate about creating desserts that deserve center-stage and make you smile; creating special moments that will stay with you forever.

My love for baking began after looking for a creative outlet, to de-stress in the evenings and on the weekends. what started out as baking for friends and family, turned into a passion. I love being able to create my customers' vision in each dessert design; and receiving amazing reactions from my customers, which makes doing all this worthwhile.

As a small business, I am extremely grateful to all of my customers and love building relationships to help me get to know you better and design the perfect dessert for you, your family and friends.
